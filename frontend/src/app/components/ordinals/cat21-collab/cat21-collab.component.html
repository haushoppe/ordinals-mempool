Introducing CAT-21 ordinals

CAT-21 is a new protocol that utilizes Bitcoin transactions and the Ordinals Theory to mint unique digital collectibles - CAT-21 ordinals. Each CAT-21 ordinal is linked to unique pixelated cat image with various traits, offering a fresh and playful perspective on Bitcoin transactions and the Ordinals theory. Why? Mint mechanics

CAT-21 ordinals are free. Completely free. Like beer. There is no pre-allocation and there are no hidden fees. You only pay the fees that go to the miner. We worked super hard to make the protocol as efficient as possible. To rescue one cat you only have to pay the fee for one Bitcoin standard transactions ‚Äì which is around 2-3 USD in Bitcoin. The CAT-21 protocol is unstoppable and can generate a potential unlimited amount of unique cats. So there is no hard-cap on the collection size. But all cats are indexed by our indexer and every cat gets a incremented number. You don't want to miss you chance to get a sub 1k, 5k, and 10k cat! Don't you?
